We are Sofa

Sofa is a software and interaction design company. We develop products and help others design theirs.

  • Products

    Effective software starts with a good user experience. We strive to delight people with beautiful UI and solid interaction models in our products.

  • Work

    Besides creating our own products, we help others to design and refine theirs. From mockup or protorype to complete products. Or anything in between.

  • Company

    Sofa was founded late 2006 and is based in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. We are small, privately held company that specializes in interaction design.

  • Blog

    Every day we find out something new, or sometimes, something old. Our blog is where we keep track of thoughts that stuck long enough to write them down.
